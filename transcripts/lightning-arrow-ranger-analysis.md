### 1. CORE PRINCIPLES

- **Opportunity cost is everything.** Being 15 minutes faster into maps matters more than being overleveled. Skip questionable XP mobs and arrive at good zones sooner rather than grinding bad zones to be comfortable. (00:26:00)
- **Splits lie.** A 17-minute Merveil with level 11 and no gear is worse than a 19-minute Merveil at level 13 with three transmutes and a three-link ready for Act 2. Context matters more than raw split times. (00:50:17)
- **Bows scale off weapon, not character.** The entire run's pace is dictated by bow quality. The difference between a good and bad bow run IS the difference between a good and bad run overall. (00:14:09)
- **Three-link is non-negotiable.** Your main attack link is the mandatory gear piece. Everything else -- auras, movement, utility -- is optional and can be shuffled. Never sacrifice your primary damage link for anything. (02:22:07)
- **Precise Technique requires accuracy > life.** Rangers naturally stack dexterity and have low life, making this keystone easy to maintain. Monitor accuracy vs life totals when swapping gear. (00:59:28)
- **Pick up rare items unidentified for transmute shards.** Identifying everything wastes scrolls and yields fewer transmutes. Unidentified rares vendor for transmute shards, which fund flat damage crafts later. (00:07:31)
- **Essences are your primary crafting currency.** Any flat elemental damage essence on a bow roughly doubles your damage. Prioritize using essences on bows over any other gear slot. (00:28:54)
- **Trinity requires two alternating elemental damage types.** Lightning's wide damage range naturally causes it to sometimes roll below fire/cold averages, proccing Trinity. If one element dominates too heavily, Trinity stops working. (01:59:07)
- **Resist benchmarks shift by act.** You do not need capped resists during the campaign. Aim for approximately 105% total combined resists entering Act 5, and solve resist gaps with crafting bench and essence rolls. (01:25:19)
- **Life tap is a massive damage multiplier.** Because spending life instead of mana triggers Mana Forged Arrows constantly, Life Tap effectively becomes a "more damage" support. Not socketing it is a major DPS loss. (02:24:49)

### 3. MECHANICAL OPTIMIZATIONS

- **[MAJOR]** Do not press quicksilver immediately after entering a portal/door -- duration is only ~6 seconds, wasted on short transitions. (00:08:29)
- **[MAJOR]** Equipping a chest piece costs 3% movement speed. Delay equipping chest until you need the survivability (e.g., before a boss). Remove chest during clear for speed. (00:05:56, 00:11:56)
- **[MAJOR]** Weapon swap for movement: Shield Charge in weapon swap with Momentum + Faster Attacks is dramatically faster than default walking. Buy a cheap shield from vendor. (00:10:47, 00:11:24)
- **[MAJOR]** Blood Rage will kill you running through lab traps. Turn it off before trap sections. (02:20:04)
- **[MAJOR]** 6-socket items sell for 7 jeweller's orbs, which convert to fusings, which convert to chance orbs (needed for maps). Pick up all 6-sockets. (02:03:05)

- **[MINOR]** Unidentified rare items vendor for transmute shards. Identified items vendor for alteration shards. Early game, leave rares unidentified to stockpile transmutes for flat damage crafts. (00:07:31)
- **[MINOR]** Chrome recipe: vendor items with linked R-G-B sockets for chromatic orbs. Pick up chrome items throughout. (00:20:09)
- **[MINOR]** Pure evasion bases are easiest to chrome to 3 green sockets (for bow gems). Costs 2 transmutes per attempt though, which is expensive early. (00:16:18)

- **[MINOR]** Stand behind Solaris pillar to dodge the spear throw attack in the Solaris boss fight. (04:09:39)

### 5. DECISION HEURISTICS

- **IF** you find a good bow but have no 3-link for it **THEN** carry a backup chest/helm with matching links and swap when ready -- **BECAUSE** dropping to a 1-link is unacceptable DPS loss. (00:05:56)

- **IF** you have zero transmutes by Act 2 **THEN** you are identifying too many rare items -- **BECAUSE** unidentified rares vendor for transmute shards which are more valuable than the ID chance of finding an upgrade. (00:07:31)

- **IF** you find a flat elemental damage essence **THEN** save it for a bow, not armor -- **BECAUSE** flat elemental on a bow approximately doubles your damage while on armor it does nothing comparable. (00:09:24)

- **IF** Trinity stops proccing (one element dominates) **THEN** craft the underrepresented element on your gear or swap Anger on/off -- **BECAUSE** Trinity provides massive damage only when both elements alternate as highest. (01:56:27)

- **IF** a mob pack is rare/blue with many enemies **THEN** always stop and kill it -- **BECAUSE** rare/magic packs give disproportionate XP that keeps you at level thresholds. (00:05:27, 00:11:56)

- **IF** mobs are stone golems or high-HP single targets **THEN** skip them -- **BECAUSE** time-to-kill ratio is terrible compared to clearing large packs. (00:20:41)

- **IF** you find a two-stone ring near Act 3+ **THEN** pick it up for vault corruption chamber -- **BECAUSE** resist rings solve the biggest gearing constraint in mid-campaign. (00:31:44)

- **IF** your fire resist is capped entering Crematorium **THEN** walk straight over fire traps -- **BECAUSE** capped fire res trivializes the zone. If not capped, you get "completely owned." (00:54:28)

- **IF** you are level 24 entering Docks (level 29 zone) **THEN** it is fine -- **BECAUSE** you still get ~95% XP and the zone prints XP so fast you will hit 25 before leaving. (00:27:13)

- **IF** you need endurance charge generation **THEN** prioritize unveiling rings for +1 min endurance charge on kill -- **BECAUSE** endurance charges are the primary physical mitigation for bow builds and dramatically reduce deaths. (03:08:36)

- **IF** instant mana flask or "recover mana at end" flask drops **THEN** never use it -- **BECAUSE** these are the worst possible mana flask mods for sustained combat. (00:06:53)

- **IF** you find a six-socket item in maps **THEN** always pick it up -- **BECAUSE** it converts to 7 jeweller's -> fusings -> chance orbs, which are critical for map sustain. (02:03:05)

- **IF** boss fight is dangerous and you have portals **THEN** place portal at boss entrance and use it as free resurrections -- **BECAUSE** death costs are time, not currency, in softcore speed runs. (01:50:13)
